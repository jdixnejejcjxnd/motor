HomeFit 60+

Target Group: Adults age 60+

Purpose: Maintain cardiovascular health, strength, balance, and flexibility at home.

Weekly Plan

Perform 3–5x per week. Each session lasts approximately 30 minutes.

Warm-Up (5 Minutes)

  • March in place – 2 minutes
  • Arm circles – 10 forward / 10 backward
  • Shoulder and neck rolls – 10 reps
  • Ankle circles – 10 each leg

Workouts

1. Aerobic: March in Place

3 sets of 1 minute with 30 seconds rest

2. Strength: Sit-to-Stand

3 sets of 8–10 reps

3. Strength: Wall Push-Ups

2–3 sets of 10–12 reps

4. Balance: Heel-to-Toe Walk

10 steps each way, 2 passes

5. Flexibility: Seated Hamstring Stretch

Hold 15–20 seconds per leg, repeat twice

Cool Down

2–3 minutes of deep breathing and gentle stretching.

Summary

This at-home program targets age-related physical decline with simple exercises that improve strength, balance, and flexibility. Designed for independence and long-term wellness.
